Reglazing Saves You Money

Why would you want to send your fixtures, vanities or tiles to the landfill? Our experts can reglaze them for you and make them look like brand new. And the best part is that this will cost just a fraction of the price you would pay to replace the items. Just think how much installing a fixture can cost.
