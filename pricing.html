How long does a reglazing procedure take?

Reglazing a bathtub or a Jacuzzi takes 3 to 5 hours. Smaller fixtures can be resurfaced faster.

How long should I let the surface cure for?

You should let the surface dry for at least 24 hours. We recommend 48 hours.

Does Bronx Bathtub Reglazing offer a warranty?

Yes, we do offer a warranty for your peace of mind.

Can you resurface my bathtub today?

In most cases, we are able to provide same-day reglazing services in Bronx.

Are refinishing and reglazing the same thing?

Yes, both terms refer to the process of reapplying the coating to fixtures, tiles and vanities in the kitchen or bathroom.

Can't I just use a DIY kit to reglaze my sink?

DIY kits are very limited. Unfortunately, you won't be able to achieve the same results we achieve by using professional materials and solutions.

Warranty on Commercial & Residential Services

Our warranty protects you from unforeseen problems with the coating. We will always stand behind our products and services. However, using abrasive cleaning products, ammonia, bleach, acids, and other highly aggressive substances to clean the fixture usually voids the warranty.

 

Refinisning Cleaning Instructions

To clean the reglazed fixture or tile, use soft cleaning products such as non-abrasive sponges or damp cloth. Cleaning solutions like Lysol, Formula 409, Fantastik, and Scrubbing Bubbles are safe to use. Rinse the surface thoroughly after cleaning and wipe it dry.
